About Footprint of India

Footprint of India is an online portal dedicated to showcasing the vast historical and cultural heritage of India. Dive into the stories of ancient times, explore rare artifacts, and connect with the essence of India.

History of India

India has a rich and diverse history spanning thousands of years. From ancient civilizations like the Indus Valley to the Mughal Empire and British colonization, every era has left its mark.

Ancient History

The ancient period saw the rise of the Maurya and Gupta empires, famous for their art and advancements in science.

Medieval History

During the medieval period, India flourished under dynasties like the Cholas, Mughals, and more.

Modern History

The fight for independence marked the modern era, with leaders like Mahatma Gandhi and Bhagat Singh shaping the nation's future.
